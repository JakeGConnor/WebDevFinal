This is the first Image that I was able to find from what we started working on. We started in high school and would spend some weekend nights working on cutting boards, serving trays, and some coasters. We had a lot of big ideas, but we didn't have a lot of free time between sports and extracurriculars.

We got better slowly and made what we could when we could. Cade was able to get a lot of the tools that we used from family and they were excited about it. We had plans to make it to a craft fair and finally sell some of what we were making. Then Cade got sick and has not been able to do much since then.

Covid took out a lot of my ability to even see Cade for the better part of the first 9 months or so. We weren't able to do a lot, but now that I was able to work while attending college I was able to get some upgrades. Along with helping a neighbor cut down a beautiful walnut tree, we have material for projects.

Now the future is foggy, we are creating our own peices when we have the chance and inspiration knocks. With more upgrades we are able to make better peices more efficiently. As for now we are just going to make beautiful peices when we can.
