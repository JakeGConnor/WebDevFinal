Featured Product

Live Edge End Table

This end table is truly exceptional in its design. It comes as a set, and when one is placed, the other perfectly complements it. Crafted with a solid walnut top featuring a striking natural live edge, this piece exudes elegance. The legs, constructed from robust 1.5-inch square steel, are equipped with leveling feet to ensure stability, even on uneven surfaces.

Each of these pieces boasts a spacious 12-inch long, 15-inch wide, and 3-inch deep drawer, making it ideal for storing books, tablets, wallets, or watches. This end table is impressively sturdy, with a substantial weight of nearly 70 pounds. In terms of dimensions, it stands at 24.5 inches in height, 20 inches in width, and 16 inches in depth, making it a perfect addition to any living space.



Custom Design

Live Edge End Table

The initial phase in product development entails the crucial task of crafting its design. While this stage may not consume an extensive amount of time, its significance cannot be overstated in the overall construction process.

Every detail, ranging from the selection of materials to the approximation of colors, is meticulously considered during this phase. By doing so, we can achieve precision in both dimensions and weight before embarking on any physical construction.

The custom design encompasses a multitude of factors, such as drawers, doors, materials, dimensions, and style. This comprehensive approach empowers us to transform a conceptual masterpiece into a tangible reality.
